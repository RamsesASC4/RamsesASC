NetherRealm Studios is a well-known game-developing company that has produced multiple award-winning games that are still crowd-pleasing productions

Timeline of NetherRealm Studios

2011: Mortal Kombat

This was NetherRealm's initial video game release and over 2 million copies were across both Xbox and Playstation. This game helped reinsert the popularity of the Mortal Kombat franchise.

2013: Injustice Gods Among Us

425,000 copies were sold in the first month. Following, the game went on to earn several critically-acclaimed awards such as "Game of the Year - 2013." The game was so popular that a mobile was produced for fans.

2015: Mortal Kombat X

Sold a whopping 5 million copies thus making it the fastest-selling game in 2015. The game was the first to use advanced graphic systems that made the chracters look more natural. A mobile version is also available for it.

2017: Injustice 2

In its first two weeks it became the best-selling game in May and it was already earned the award for "Best Fighting Game." A ground-breaking mobile version was also created for injustice 2 which by far has the experience due unbelievable graphics and vivid gameplay.
